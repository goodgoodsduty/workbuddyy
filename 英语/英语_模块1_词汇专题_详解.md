## 五、近义词辨析

### 5.1 say / speak / talk / tell

| 词 | 用法 |
|----|------|
| **say** | "说"的内容，后接说话内容 |
| **speak** | 说某种语言，强调能力 |
| **talk** | 交谈，强调对话 |
| **tell** | 告诉某人某事，后接人 |

**例**：
- He **said** that he was tired.
- He **speaks** English fluently.
- They are **talking** about the weather.
- He **told** me the truth.

### 5.2 affect / effect

| 词 | 词性 | 含义 |
|----|------|------|
| **affect** | 动词 | 影响 |
| **effect** | 名词 | 影响/效果 |

**例**：
- The weather **affects** my mood.
- The **effect** of the medicine is immediate.

### 5.3 rise / raise

| 词 | 用法 |
|----|------|
| **rise** | 不及物，自身上升 |
| **raise** | 及物，使…上升 |

**例**：
- The sun **rises** in the east.
- He **raised** his hand.

### 5.4 lie / lay

| 词 | 用法 |
|----|------|
| **lie** | 躺，位于（不及物）|
| **lay** | 放，产卵（及物）|

**例**：
- He is **lying** on the bed.
- Please **lay** the book on the table.

### 5.5 borrow / lend

| 词 | 含义 |
|----|------|
| **borrow** | 借入（向他人）|
| **lend** | 借出（给他人）|

**例**：
- Can I **borrow** your pen?
- Can you **lend** me your pen?

## 八、词汇练习（10题精练）

### 练习1
翻译：He is **accustomed to** working overtime.
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

accustomed to = 习惯于

翻译：他习惯于加班。

</details>

### 练习2
选择：The weather will _____ our picnic.
A. affect B. effect C. affect to D. effect to
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**A. affect**（影响，动词）

</details>

### 练习3
填空：The sun _____ in the east.
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

rises（不及物动词，自身上升）

</details>

### 练习4
翻译：除了英语，他还学法语。
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

In addition to English, he also studies French.

</details>

### 练习5
选择：Can I _____ your bike?
A. lend B. borrow C. lend you D. lend to you
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. borrow**（借入）

</details>

### 练习6
翻译：尽管他很累，他还是完成了工作。
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

Although he was tired, he still finished the work.
（或：Despite his tiredness, he finished the work.）

</details>

### 练习7
选择：I am looking forward to _____ from you.
A. hear B. hearing C. be hearing D. heard
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. hearing**（look forward to + doing）

</details>

### 练习8
用adapt / adopt 填空：
(1) He decided to _____ a new policy.
(2) It takes time to _____ to a new environment.
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

(1) **adopt**（采纳）
(2) **adapt**（适应）

</details>

### 练习9
翻译：The book is worth _____.
A. read B. reading C. to read D. be read
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. reading**（be worth doing 主动表被动）

</details>

### 练习10
翻译：He is good at English, _____ his sister.
<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

as well as（和…一样好）

完整：He is good at English as well as his sister.
（他和他姐姐一样擅长英语。）

</details>
