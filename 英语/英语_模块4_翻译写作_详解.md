## 二、英译汉（15分）⚠️ 必考

### 2.1 评分标准

| 项目 | 分值 | 评分点 |
|------|------|--------|
| 准确度 | 60% | 关键词、句意正确 |
| 通顺度 | 30% | 中文表达流畅 |
| 完整度 | 10% | 无漏译 |

### 2.2 翻译三大原则

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **信** | 准确表达原文意思 |
| **达** | 译文通顺 |
| **雅** | 用词优雅（次要）|

### 2.3 翻译基本步骤 ⚠️

| 步骤 | 操作 |
|------|------|
| 1. 断句 | 找主谓宾 |
| 2. 译骨架 | 先译主干 |
| 3. 加修饰 | 后译定状补 |
| 4. 调整语序 | 按中文习惯调 |
| 5. 检查 | 关键词是否译对 |

### 2.4 常见考点 ⚠️

| 考点 | 例 |
|------|---|
| 定语从句 | The book (that) I bought... |
| 被动语态 | ...is used to... |
| 虚拟语气 | If I were you, ... |
| 倒装句 | Never have I... |
| 固定搭配 | be accustomed to, look forward to |
| 长难句 | 多重修饰 |

### 2.5 翻译技巧

**【增译法】** 英文省略的部分中文补上
- I like swimming. → 我喜欢（去）游泳。

**【减译法】** 英文的代词/冠词中文省略
- I have a book. → 我有一本书。

**【词性转换】** 名词↔动词、形容词↔动词
- I am a teacher. → 我是一名老师。/ 我教书。
- The book is interesting. → 这本书有趣。

**【语序调整】** 后置定语→前置
- the book on the table → 桌上的书
- the man who came yesterday → 昨天来的那个人

**【被动→主动】**
- The book was written by him. → 这本书是他写的。

### 2.6 翻译练习

**【例题1】** 翻译：The book that I bought yesterday is very interesting.

**参考译文**：我昨天买的那本书很有趣。

解析：that I bought yesterday 是定语从句，翻译时前置

**【例题2】** 翻译：It is widely acknowledged that exercise contributes to physical and mental health.

**参考译文**：人们普遍认为锻炼有益于身心健康。

解析：It is... that... 是主语从句，翻译时调整语序

**【例题3】** 翻译：Had I known about the meeting, I would have attended it.

**参考译文**：如果我早知道有这个会议，我就会参加了。

解析：倒装句还原为 If I had known... 句式

**【例题4】** 翻译：The number of students who take online courses has increased dramatically.

**参考译文**：参加在线课程的学生数量大幅增加了。

**【例题5】** 翻译：Despite the fact that he is busy, he always finds time to read.

**参考译文**：尽管他很忙，他总是抽时间读书。

## 三、写作（15分）⚠️ 必考

### 3.1 评分标准

| 档次 | 分值 | 特征 |
|------|------|------|
| 1档 | 13-15分 | 内容完整、语法准确、表达流畅 |
| 2档 | 9-12分 | 内容较完整、有少量错误 |
| 3档 | 5-8分 | 内容不完整、错误较多 |
| 4档 | 1-4分 | 大量错误、不知所云 |

### 3.2 写作要求

| 项目 | 要求 |
|------|------|
| 字数 | 100-150词 |
| 词数 | 不少于100词 |
| 体裁 | 议论文/说明文/应用文 |
| 内容 | 完整、有条理 |

### 3.3 写作三段式结构 ⚠️ 必背

```
第一段（3-4句）：引出话题 + 表明观点
第二段（5-7句）：展开论述 + 举例说明
第三段（2-3句）：总结观点 + 展望/建议
```

### 3.4 万能模板 ⚠️ 必背

**【开头】**

模板A（现象引入法）：
```
In recent years, ... has become a hot topic.
With the development of ..., ... is becoming more and more popular.
As we all know, ... plays an important role in our life.
```

模板B（观点引入法）：
```
People's views on ... vary from person to person.
Some people believe that ..., while others argue that ...
As far as I'm concerned, ...
```

**【主体】**

模板：
```
First of all, ... Moreover, ... In addition, ...
For example, ...
There is no doubt that ...
```

**【结尾】**

模板A：
```
In conclusion, I firmly believe that ...
Only in this way can we ...
```

模板B：
```
To sum up, ... should be ...
As a college student, we should ...
```

**【科技类】**

**【环境类】**

### 3.6 常用句型 ⚠️ 必背

**【开头】**
1. Nowadays, ... has become a common phenomenon in our daily life.
2. As the society develops rapidly, ... is becoming increasingly popular.
3. There is a heated discussion about ... in modern society.

**【论证】**
1. First and foremost, ... is of great importance.
2. What is more, ... can help us to ...
3. In addition, ... plays a vital role in ...
4. For example, ...
5. A case in point is that ...
6. As a matter of fact, ...

**【结尾】**
1. In conclusion, I am convinced that ...
2. All in all, it is high time that we should ...
3. Only by ... can we ...

### 3.7 写作练习

**【题目】** Some people think college students should take part-time jobs, while others disagree. What's your opinion? Write a composition in about 120 words.

**【参考范文】**

Nowadays, whether college students should take part-time jobs has aroused wide concern. People hold different views on this issue.

Some people believe that taking part-time jobs can help students gain practical experience and develop their social skills. Moreover, earning money can reduce the financial burden on their families.

However, others argue that part-time jobs may distract students from their studies. They believe that academic performance should be the top priority for college students.

As for me, I am in favor of taking part-time jobs moderately. On the one hand, it can provide valuable experience. On the other hand, students should balance work and study well. Only by doing this can we benefit from part-time jobs.

**（词数：约140词）**

### 3.8 写作常见错误

| 错误 | 正确 |
|------|------|
| 主谓不一致 | He go → He goes |
| 时态错误 | Yesterday I go → Yesterday I went |
| 介词错误 | discuss about → discuss |
| 固定搭配错误 | make the bed (做) / do homework (做) |
| 拼写错误 | recieve → receive |
| 中文式英语 | I very like → I like very much |

## 四、写作素材积累

### 4.1 万能过渡词

| 类型 | 词 |
|------|---|
| 顺序 | First, Second, Third, Finally |
| 递进 | Moreover, Furthermore, In addition, What's more |

| 总结 | In conclusion, To sum up, All in all |

### 4.2 万能句式

**【原因型】**
- The reason for ... is that ...
- ... is attributed to ...
- ... is responsible for ...

**【比较型】**
- Compared with ..., ... has more advantages.
- ... is superior to ... in ...

**【观点型】**
- As far as I'm concerned, ...

- In my opinion, ...

### 练习6（写作）
题目：The Importance of Reading Books
<details>
<summary>🔑 参考范文</summary>

In recent years, with the development of technology, many people tend to watch videos rather than read books. However, reading books is still of great importance in our life.

First and foremost, reading books can broaden our horizons. Through reading, we can learn about different cultures, histories, and ideas that we cannot experience in our daily life. Moreover, reading also helps improve our thinking ability and language skills. For example, regular reading can enhance our vocabulary and writing ability.

In conclusion, reading books is an essential part of our life. As college students, we should develop the habit of reading and benefit from it.

</details>

### 练习7（写作）
题目：How to Keep Healthy
<details>
<summary>🔑 参考范文</summary>

Nowadays, more and more people are paying attention to their health. As college students, we should also learn how to keep healthy.

There are several ways to keep healthy. First, we should take regular exercise, such as running, swimming, or playing ball games. Exercise can not only strengthen our body but also relieve stress. Second, we should have a balanced diet, eating more vegetables and fruits while reducing junk food. Third, we need to have enough sleep, at least 7-8 hours every day. Finally, maintaining a positive attitude is also important for our health.

In a word, keeping healthy requires us to develop good living habits. Let's start from now and live a healthy life.

</details>

### 练习8（写作）
题目：The Advantages of Online Learning
<details>
<summary>🔑 参考范文</summary>

With the development of the Internet, online learning has become increasingly popular, especially during the past few years.

Online learning has many advantages. Firstly, it is very convenient. We can take courses anytime and anywhere as long as we have a computer and the Internet. Secondly, it provides us with abundant learning resources. We can choose the courses that interest us and learn at our own pace. Thirdly, online learning is cost-effective, saving us time and money on commuting.

However, online learning also has its drawbacks, such as the lack of face-to-face interaction and the need for self-discipline. In my opinion, online learning is a good supplement to traditional education. As college students, we should make good use of online resources to improve ourselves.

</details>

### 练习9（写作）
题目：My Hobby
<details>
<summary>🔑 参考范文</summary>

Everyone has his or her own hobby. As for me, my hobby is reading, which has been a part of my life since I was a child.

There are several reasons why I love reading. First, reading opens a window to the world. Through books, I can travel to different places and learn about various cultures. Second, reading enriches my knowledge and broadens my horizons. Third, reading helps me relax after a busy day.

To pursue my hobby, I usually spend an hour reading before going to bed. I have also joined the school reading club, where I can share my thoughts with other book lovers.

In conclusion, reading is not only my hobby but also my lifelong companion. I believe that with books, my life will be more colorful.

</details>

### 练习10（写作）
题目：The Importance of Time Management
<details>
<summary>🔑 参考范文</summary>

Time is one of the most valuable resources in our life. As college students, learning to manage time effectively is crucial for our success.

Good time management brings many benefits. First, it helps us improve our efficiency. By planning our day in advance, we can accomplish more in less time. Second, it reduces stress. When we have a clear schedule, we feel more in control. Third, it allows us to balance study, work, and leisure, making our life more fulfilling.

To manage time well, we should set clear goals, make a to-do list, and avoid procrastination. We can also use apps to track our progress.

In a word, mastering time management is a key skill that will benefit us throughout our life. Let's start to manage our time wisely from today.

</details>
