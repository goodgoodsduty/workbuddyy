## 三、答题方法 ⚠️ 必考

### 3.1 通用答题流程

```
1. 读题目（带问题读）
2. 划关键词
3. 回原文定位
4. 找对应信息
5. 排除干扰项
6. 选出答案
```

### 3.2 时间分配

| 阶段 | 时间 |
|------|------|
| 读题+定位 | 1-2分钟 |
| 精读段落 | 3-4分钟 |
| 做题+检查 | 2-3分钟 |
| 合计 | 8-10分钟 |

## 四、细节题（占60%）⚠️ 必考

### 4.1 特征

- 题干有具体信息
- 答案在原文中能找到对应
- 选项往往是同义替换

### 4.2 解题技巧 ⚠️

| 步骤 | 操作 |
|------|------|
| 1. 划题干关键词 | 找具体人名、数字、时间 |
| 2. 回原文定位 | 找对应段落和句子 |
| 3. 比对选项 | 找同义替换 |
| 4. 排除干扰 | 与原文矛盾/无中生有/偷换概念 |

### 4.3 干扰项特征 ⚠️ 必记

| 类型 | 例 |
|------|---|
| 无中生有 | 选项内容原文没提 |
| 偷换概念 | 张冠李戴、时态/对象变化 |
| 范围扩大/缩小 | partial → all, sometimes → always |
| 答非所问 | 看似相关但答错问题 |

**【例题1】**（细节题）
原文：The library opens at 8 a.m. on weekdays, but on weekends it opens at 9 a.m.

问题：When does the library open on Sundays?
A. At 8 a.m.  B. At 9 a.m.  C. At 10 a.m.  D. At 7 a.m.

**答案**：**B. At 9 a.m.**

解析：weekend 包括 Sunday，所以是 9 a.m.

---

## 五、推断题（占20%）⚠️ 必考

### 5.1 特征

- 题干有 infer / imply / suggest / conclude
- 答案不是原文直接说，而是"言外之意"
- 选项有一定相似度

### 5.2 解题技巧

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 不能过度推断 | 答案必须能从原文推出 |
| 不能字面理解 | 要理解深层含义 |
| 不能无中生有 | 必须有原文依据 |
| 排除极端选项 | often, all, never 多为错误 |

### 5.3 常见推理方向

| 方向 | 例子 |
|------|------|
| 因果推断 | 现象→原因 |
| 态度推断 | 作者看法 |
| 情感推断 | 人物情感 |
| 后续推断 | 接下来会发生什么 |

**【例题2】**（推断题）
原文：Tom has been working overtime for a week. He looks tired and sometimes falls asleep at his desk.

问题：What can we infer about Tom?
A. He likes his job
B. He is exhausted
C. He doesn't eat well
D. He will quit his job

**答案**：**B. He is exhausted**

解析：从"overtime for a week", "looks tired", "falls asleep" 可推断他很累。

---

## 六、主旨题（占10%）⚠️ 必考

### 6.1 特征

- 题干有 main idea / mainly about / title / best title
- 考察对全文/段落大意的把握
- 选项常常"以偏概全"或"过度引申"

### 6.2 解题技巧

| 位置 | 注意 |
|------|------|
| 文章首段 | 主题句常在开头 |
| 文章末段 | 总结性句子 |
| 每段开头 | 段意概括 |
| 每段结尾 | 段意总结 |

### 6.3 干扰项特征

| 类型 | 例 |
|------|---|
| 范围太小 | 只讲某个细节 |
| 范围太大 | 超出文章讨论范围 |
| 不相关 | 与主题无关 |
| 错位 | 与原文观点相反 |

**【例题3】**（主旨题）
原文讲了几个解决城市交通拥堵的方案：扩建道路、发展公共交通、鼓励步行骑车。

问题：The main idea of the passage is _____.
A. Why cities have traffic problems
B. The history of urban transportation
C. Solutions to urban traffic congestion
D. The importance of cars

**答案**：**C. Solutions to urban traffic congestion**

---

## 七、词义题（占10%）⚠️ 必考

### 7.1 特征

- 题干有：The word "X" in line Y most probably means ____.
- 考察对上下文词义的理解

### 7.2 解题技巧 ⚠️

| 方法 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 上下文线索 | 同义、反义、解释 |
| 构词法 | 词根词缀 |
| 逻辑关系 | 因果、对比、并列 |
| 代入验证 | 选项代入原文读 |

### 7.3 三大线索 ⚠️

| 线索 | 标志词 | 关系 |
|------|--------|------|
| **解释** | that is, i.e., or, in other words | 同义 |
| **反义** | but, however, although, on the contrary | 反义 |
| **并列** | and, or, also, as well as | 同类 |

**【例题4】**（词义题）
原文：The company is facing a financial crisis. They are on the **brink** of bankruptcy.

问题：The word "brink" most probably means _____.
A. edge  B. middle  C. beginning  D. end

**答案**：**A. edge**（brink = 边缘）

解析：on the brink of = on the edge of "在…的边缘"

## 八、阅读长难句分析方法

### 8.1 三步法 ⚠️

| 步骤 | 操作 |
|------|------|
| 1. 找主干 | 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 |
| 2. 找修饰 | 定语/状语/补语 |
| 3. 翻译 | 先主干后修饰 |

### 8.2 找主干三剑客

| 标志 | 作用 |
|------|------|
| **断句号**（. ; ? !） | 分隔句子 |
| **连接词**（and, but, which） | 切分主从句 |
| **引导词**（that, who, which, when） | 找从句起点 |

**【例题5】** 拆解长难句
"The book that I bought yesterday, which was written by a famous author, is very interesting."

主干：The book **is** very interesting
定语1：that I bought yesterday（修饰 book）
定语2：which was written by a famous author（修饰 book）

## 九、阅读提速技巧 ⚠️

### 9.1 不要逐字读

- 抓**段意**和**关键句**
- 跳过无关细节
- 关注转折词、因果词

### 9.2 关键词标志

| 词 | 含义 |
|----|------|
| **but / however** | 转折，重点在后 |
| **therefore / so** | 因果，重点在后 |
| **for example** | 例证，例子不重要 |
| **in fact / actually** | 强调，重点在后 |
| **first / then / finally** | 顺序 |

### 9.3 不要先读文章再读题

正确顺序：**先读题 → 划关键词 → 回原文定位**

## 十一、配套练习（5篇阅读）

### Passage 1（细节题+主旨题）

The Internet has changed the way we live, work, and communicate. With just a few clicks, we can shop, learn, and connect with people around the world. However, this convenience comes with risks. Privacy concerns, online scams, and the spread of misinformation are growing problems. Experts suggest that users should be cautious about sharing personal information and should verify sources before trusting online content.

1. According to the passage, what is one risk of using the Internet?
A. Higher prices
B. Privacy concerns
C. Slow connection
D. Bad weather

<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. Privacy concerns**

</details>

2. What is the main idea of the passage?
A. The history of the Internet
B. The benefits and risks of the Internet
C. How to use computers
D. The future of technology

<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. The benefits and risks of the Internet**

</details>

### Passage 2（推断题+词义题）

Many young people today are choosing to delay marriage and have children later in life. This trend is often attributed to economic factors, such as the high cost of housing and education, as well as changing social attitudes. **Consequently**, many governments are concerned about declining birth rates and the long-term impact on the economy.

3. What does the word "Consequently" mean?
A. However
B. As a result
C. For example
D. In contrast

<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. As a result**（因此）

</details>

4. What can we infer about the trend of delaying marriage?
A. It is popular among older people
B. It will continue due to economic factors
C. It is illegal in some countries
D. It has no impact on society

<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. It will continue due to economic factors**

</details>

### Passage 3（细节题）

Studies show that people who exercise regularly have better mental health than those who don't. Exercise releases endorphins, which are natural mood lifters. It also helps reduce stress and anxiety. Experts recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise, such as walking or cycling, five times a week.

5. According to the passage, how often should people exercise?
A. Every day
B. Five times a week
C. Once a week
D. Twice a month

<details>
<summary>🔑 答案</summary>

**B. Five times a week**

</details>
